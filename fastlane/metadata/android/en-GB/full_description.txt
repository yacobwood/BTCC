BTCC Hub is the ultimate companion app for British Touring Car Championship fans.

Stay on top of every race weekend with live standings, full results from every session (Practice, Qualifying, Races 1, 2 and 3), and the complete 2026 season calendar with venue details, track facts and lap records.

WHAT'S INSIDE
- Live race radio - tune in to BTCC commentary live from the circuit
- Race results - full grid positions, grid deltas and fastest laps for every session
- Driver profiles - career histories, stats and championship records for every driver on the 2026 grid
- Team standings - follow the manufacturers' battle alongside the drivers' championship
- BTCC News - the latest articles direct from btcc.net
- Season calendar - all 10 rounds with dates, circuit info and track records
- Podcasts - the official BTCC podcast, filterable by session type
- TOCA Radio - the official championship radio stream
- Home screen widgets - next race countdown in small, medium and large sizes
- All-time records - career stats across every BTCC season dating back to 2004

Designed for fans who follow every round. No clutter - just the race data you actually want.
